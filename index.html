Welcome to Escape for a Cause!

Escape for a Cause is a unique charity event where teams of 3-5 people will compete in a series of exciting escape rooms. The goal is to be the first team to escape all the rooms and win the grand prize. By participating in or donating to this event, you'll not only have a chance to test your problem-solving skills and teamwork, but you'll also be supporting a good cause. All proceeds from the event will go towards supporting a local charity.

How Can I Participate?

If you're interested in participating in the escape room challenge, simply visit the Sign Up page and fill out the form. You'll need to form a team of 3-5 people and come up with a team name. If you'd like to support the event but don't want to participate, you can donate to watch the event live. We'll also be streaming the event online so that you can follow all the action from the comfort of your own home.

What's at stake?

The winning team will receive a special prize package and the bragging rights of being the first team to escape all the rooms. In addition, all participants and donors will be entered into a raffle for a chance to win additional prizes. Every little bit helps, and we hope to see you at Escape for a Cause!

About the Escape Rooms

Our escape rooms are designed to be challenging but also fun for all ages. We have a variety of themes to choose from, including a haunted house, a spy mission, and a time travel adventure. Each room has its own set of clues and puzzles that you'll need to solve in order to escape. You'll have to work together as a team and use your creativity and critical thinking skills to succeed. Are you up for the challenge?

Supporting a Good Cause

By participating in or donating to Escape for a Cause, you'll be supporting a local charity that is making a difference in your community. We believe that everyone has the power to make a positive impact, and we're grateful for your support. Thank you for helping us make a difference!

Sponsorship Opportunities

Are you a business owner looking to support a good cause and gain exposure for your company? We welcome sponsorships from businesses of all sizes. There are various levels of sponsorship available, each with its own benefits. As a sponsor, you'll receive recognition on our website and social media platforms, as well as at the event itself. You'll also have the opportunity to showcase your products or services to a captive audience. For more information on sponsorship opportunities, please contact us.

Stay Up to Date

Want to stay up to date on the latest news and updates for Escape for a Cause? Follow us on social media and sign up for our newsletter. We'll keep you informed about event details, registration deadlines, and more. We can't wait to see you at the event!
